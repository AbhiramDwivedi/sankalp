# Content Band Differentiation Audit

Spot-check across 6 packs (3 from L1 / Foundations, 3 from L2 / Intermediate) to confirm the hand-authored content actually steps up in difficulty between bands rather than being cosmetically rebadged.

## Sample

| Pack | Band | Vocab items | Grammar focus | Model essay 1 word count | Connectors used |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| L1-01 greetings | Foundations | 29 | Copula होना (present), register आप/तुम/तू, possessive agreement | 121 | 4 (पहले, लेकिन, क्योंकि, इसके बाद) |
| L1-05 numbers-time | Foundations | 27 | Numbers, clock-time pattern, present-habit verbs | 132 | 7 |
| L1-12 restaurants-food | Foundations | 27 | Postpositions, count nouns, oblique case | 124 | 3 |
| L2-01 daily-routine | Intermediate | 30 | Reflexive routine verbs, time adverbs, sequencing | 142 | 9 |
| L2-06 health-fitness | Intermediate | 29 | Modals (चाहिए, सकना), opinion frames, conditional | 147 | 7 (incl. opinion frames "मुझे लगता है कि") |
| L2-11 countries-directions | Intermediate | 30 | Source-destination X से Y तक, compass directions, imperative -इए | 148 | 6 (incl. जबकि, अगले साल जब) |

## Findings

**Word count steps up cleanly.** L1 model essays cluster at 119-132 words; L2 model essays sit at 142-148 words — a 15-25 word lift that mirrors the STAMP B5-vs-B5+ expectation (more elaboration, more "because/while/although" subordination, not just longer sentences).

**Connector density grows.** L1 packs use 3-7 simple-sequence connectors (पहले, फिर, लेकिन, क्योंकि). L2 packs add subordinators (जबकि, जब... तब, अगर... तो) and opinion frames (मुझे लगता है कि, मेरा मानना है कि) — exactly the language-control axis that pushes B5 → B6.

**Grammar focus differentiates by complexity, not just count.** Both bands carry 3 grammar items per pack, but L1 covers structural foundations (copula, agreement, register) while L2 covers modals, conditionals, source-destination postposition pairs, and imperative forms. This is the right shape: count is consistent so packs feel equally "weighty," but the cognitive load shifts upward.

**Vocabulary count is intentionally flat (~27-30 per pack)** across both bands. Differentiation is in the *type* of word (concrete nouns at L1, abstract opinion words and verb-pair vocabulary at L2), not the count — a defensible house-style choice for a 30-min-per-pack pacing target.

**All 6 sampled packs predict B5** in their VerdictCard. That is intentional (every pack must be capable of B5 output if pushed) but means the visible difference is in the *complexity ceiling* of the model essays, not the predicted score.

## Verdict

Content meaningfully differentiates between bands along three axes (essay length, connector sophistication, grammar complexity). L1 → L2 is not cosmetic. No content edits recommended from this audit.
